关于分散投资

wide diversification is only required when investors don't understand what they are doing. -- warren buffer.

formula: sr(multi) = C alpha sqrt(bets). sr = sharpe ratio.

alpha代表对bet了解程度. 如果alpha很高, 那么少做几次bet是值得的. 可是如果对于bet了解很少, 那么alpha很低, 所以需要尽可能地做多bet, 也就是wide diversification.

see more: http://augmentedtrader.com/2011/01/15/warren-buffet-and-the-fundamental-law/

-----

CAPM(Capital Assets Pricing Model). R(i, t) = Beta(i) * Rm(t) + Alpha(i). 其中i标识具体stock. beta则表示这个stock随市场变化的相关系数. 而alpha则是stock独立于市场变化的表现.
